Book in 4 Easy Steps

Book Your Pet's Grooming Session

Pawlendar makes pet grooming simple. Where every furbaby deserves their own spa day!

Step 1

Log In or Create an Account

Sign in to your Pawlendar account or create one to access your pets, appointments, and booking history.

Step 2

Choose Your Pet & Grooming Service

Choose one of your registered pets and select the grooming service or package you'd like to book.

Step 3

Choose a Schedule

Select an available date and time. Pawlendar automatically assigns an available groomer for your appointment.

Step 4

Confirm Appointment

Review your appointment details and confirm your booking. You'll receive updates as your appointment progresses..
