Let your furbabies take a paw-se

At Pawlendar, we combine love for animals with modern technology to deliver a seamless, stress-free grooming coordination system for pet parents and groomers alike.

How Pawlendar Started

Founded in 2026, Pawlendar began with a simple observation: scheduling pet grooming shouldn't be harder than finding a premium treat. Traditional calling, long wait lines, and uncoordinated pet medical/sensitivity updates often turned a simple bath into a complex chore.

We built Pawlendar to treat your furry friends like family. Our platform brings clarity, ease, and elite care directly to your fingertips, transforming standard grooming routines into relaxing spa experiences.
